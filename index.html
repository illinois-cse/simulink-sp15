    A Short Course for Engineers

    April 20, 22, 27, 29 (Mon/Wed) • 1–3 p.m. • MEL 1001

    MathWorks Simulink is the premier is a high-powered graphical programming language for modeling and simulating dynamic systems across many domains. This short course is designed to introduce you to the fundamentals of the interface and modeling through engineering exercises and physical models.
